What is Panchakarma?

Panchakarma is the cornerstone of Ayurvedic medicine. It is a unique, natural, holistic, health-giving series of therapeutic treatments that cleanse the body's deep tissues of toxins, open the subtle channels, and bring life-enhancing energy.

At Dhanwantari, our Panchakarma suite includes Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, and Raktamokshana, administered by certified practitioners in a serene environment.
