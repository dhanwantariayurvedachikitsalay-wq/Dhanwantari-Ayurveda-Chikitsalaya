Swarnaprashana

An Immunity Booster and Brain Development Formula

Swarnaprashana is a well-known Ayurvedic formulation recognized for its powerful immunizing properties. It is a safe and natural preparation that supports the overall growth and development of children.

This formulation contains purified gold in a bioavailable form, combined with traditional Ayurvedic ingredients known for enhancing immunity and promoting brain function.

Traditionally administered on the day of Pushya Nakshatra each month.
