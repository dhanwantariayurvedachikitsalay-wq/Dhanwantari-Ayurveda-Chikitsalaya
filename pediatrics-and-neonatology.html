Swarnaprashana

An Immunity Booster and Brain Development Formula

Suvarnaprashana is a well-known Ayurvedic formulation recognized for its powerful immunizing properties. It is a safe and natural preparation that supports the overall growth and development of children.

This formulation contains purified gold in a bioavailable form, combined with traditional Ayurvedic ingredients known for enhancing immunity and promoting brain function.

For maximum effectiveness, Suvarnaprashana is traditionally administered on the day of Pushya Nakshatra each month.

Benefits of Suvarnaprashana

  • Increases intelligence, digestive power, and physical strength
  • Acts as a longevity enhancer and health protector
  • Invigorates the nervous system with Gold & Ghee
  • Increases natural skin radiance
  • Significantly improves memory power
  • Boosts immunity against seasonal diseases

Prevention of Common Childhood Problems:

  • Recurring cold, cough, fever, and asthma
  • Digestive issues like vomiting and diarrhea
  • Lack of concentration in studies
  • Bedwetting
  • Skin related ailments
